How it works

Three simple steps, a few minutes a day.

STEP ONE

Choose your place

Pick your country or a trip you are planning. BeakBrain shows you the birds you are most likely to meet there.

STEP TWO

Play a quick round

Match photos and calls in a few minutes a day. Every bird you miss becomes a small lesson right away.

STEP THREE

Remember for good

BeakBrain brings birds back at the right moment, so the ones you learn today stay with you tomorrow.

Why it works

Common questions

Is BeakBrain free?

Yes, you can start for free. Every quick game and a starter pack for every country are always free. A Pro upgrade unlocks the full packs, seasons, trips, and offline use.

Which birds does it cover?

Birds from around the world. Packs are ordered by what is common where you are, so the first birds you learn are the ones you are most likely to see near home or on a trip.

Do I need an internet connection?

You can learn online right away. Offline use is part of Pro, so you can study on a plane or out in the field with no signal.

Is it on iPhone and Android?

BeakBrain is coming to both. Join the waitlist and we will tell you the moment it is ready to download.

Where do the photos and sounds come from?

From open, credited sources such as iNaturalist and the Xeno-canto community. Every photo and recording shows the person who made it and its licence.
