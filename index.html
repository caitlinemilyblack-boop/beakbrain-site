How it works

Short daily rounds that actually stick

Step one

Pick your country

Choose where you are birding. BeakBrain orders every bird by how common it is where you are, so you start with the ones you will really meet.

Step two

Learn by sight and sound

Identify a bird from a photo or its call. Review lookalikes side by side. A daily streak keeps you coming back.

Step three

Keep what you learn

The birds you find tricky return more often until you know them. Spaced practice does the remembering for you.

Common questions

Good to know

Is BeakBrain free?

Yes, you can start for free. Every quick game and a starter pack for every country are always free. A Pro upgrade opens the full packs, seasons, trips, and offline use.

Which birds does it cover?

Birds from every country. Packs are ordered by how common each bird is where you are, so the first birds you learn are the ones you are most likely to see near home or on a trip.

Do I need an internet connection?

You can learn online right away. Offline use is part of Pro, so you can study on a plane or out in the field with no signal.

Is it on iPhone and Android?

BeakBrain is coming to both. Join the waitlist and we will tell you the day it is ready to download.

Where do the photos and sounds come from?

From open, credited sources including Wikimedia Commons, iNaturalist and the Xeno-canto community. Every photo and recording shows the person who made it and its licence.
